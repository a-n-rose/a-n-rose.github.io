About

I have a B.A. in psychology, a M.Sc. in clinical linguistics, and I am a guest dancer in a Berlin-based dance company. It took me a while to narrow it down, but I have a mild obsession with sound and what we humans can do with it.

In Finland, 2012 - the first semester of my master's program EMCL - a professor introduced me to acoustics and programming. Since then I use programming as my stage to learn, build, play, and teach about acoustics and beyond.
